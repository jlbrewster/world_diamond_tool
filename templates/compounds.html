Diamond Polishing Compounds

World Diamond Tool offers the highest quality diamond compound available for rapid, accurate lapping, polishing and super-finishing.

Our diamonds have been tested for purity, quality, shape, and accuracy of sizing. They uniformly dispersed in our oil and water soluble lubricant.

Our diamond polishing compounds are color-coded for easy identification. Water may be used as an extender or thinner for water soluble compound, and kerosene or a light oil for oil soluble compound.

Diamond polishing & lapping compounds are available in screw-top jars or
syringes. The screw-top jar keeps diamond compound pure and permits the addition of
thinner or lubricant before use if desired. Syringe is a disposable plastic
dispenser that is convenient, and safe from outside impurities.
