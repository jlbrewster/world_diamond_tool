Commutator Turning Tools

 

 

Commutator Turning

Diamond tools are used for turning the commutators of fractional horsepower electric motors, such as motors used for vacuum cleaners, electric power tools, etc. 

The tool is traversed at a feed rate of .0005 to .0015 per revolution, taking a depth of cut from .001 to .005.   A controlled finish can be produced.
